bcmolt_inni_fec_modes inni_fec

The FEC mode desired for this INNI port.

  1. None - no FEC
  2. FC FEC - FEC as defined in IEEE 802.3 Clause 74
  3. RS FEC - FEC as defined in IEEE 802.3 Clause 91 (10G) and Clause 108 (25G).
  4. Auto negotiation - allow the link partnets to auto negotiate the FEC mode.
bcmolt_inni_lanes available_lanes

The lane protection scheme use with INNI redundancy. The options are 1.) None, 2.) All Lanes, and 3.) Even Lanes only.

bcmolt_gpio_pin ps_gpio

GPIO assigned to drive active/standby status when the Internal NNI GPIO protection is enabled.

bcmolt_gpio_polarity gpio_polarity

Polarity of the GPIO pin.

active_low asserts that the GPIO active (value 0) should drive the primary internal NNI active.

active_high asserts that the GPIO active (value 1) should drive the primary internal NNI active.
