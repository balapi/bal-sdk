bcmolt_onu_power_management_configuration Struct Reference

Detailed Description

Configuration parameters for the ONU power management feature. These should match the parameters sent to the ONU via OMCI.

Field Documentation

uint32_t ilowpower

The maximum amount of time any ONU on this NI may spend in a low power state in units of 125us.

uint32_t iaware

The minimum amount of time every ONU on his NI must spend in an aware state in units of 125us.

uint16_t itransinit

The maximum amount of time required by any ONU on this NI to power up its transmitter only from a low power state in units of 125us.

uint16_t itxinit

The maximum amount of time required by any ONU on this NI to power up its transceiver (transmitter + receiver) from a low power state in units of 125us.

uint32_t irxoff

The maximum amount of time any ONU on this NI may spend with its receiver disabled in units of 125us.
